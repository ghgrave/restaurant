Big Ben's Burgers: Our Story

Home  Menu   Specials About Us Careers

When I met my partner, I knew we were destined for great things. We both had a love for food and a comical interest in all things 'oldies.'
After years of friends joking with us about our fifties and sixties trivia, they suggested we open a restaurant. To my surprise, my partner
agreed and by the end of the following year, we were open for business.

Come visit and enjoy our daily shows at 5pm with additional entertainment Fridays and Saturdays promptly at 10pm. In the
meantime, enjoy our All-American tribute to the 50's themed diner and soda bar.

And don't forget to ask about your FREE slice of apple pie.
